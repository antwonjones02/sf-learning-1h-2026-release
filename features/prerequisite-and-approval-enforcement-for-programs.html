Prerequisite and Approval Enforcement for Programs

Feature #19 of 54

What Is This Feature?

Administrators can now enforce item-level prerequisites and approvals for courses within programs.

What Is Changing?

Programs now honor the prerequisite and approval rules set at the individual course level, enforcing proper sequencing.

Why Should We Care?

Ensures learners complete required steps in order. Prevents skipping ahead in structured development paths.

Risk of Inaction

Learners can bypass prerequisites within programs, undermining the intended learning path design.
