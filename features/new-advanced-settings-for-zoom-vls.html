New Advanced Settings for Zoom VLS

Feature #31 of 54

What Is This Feature?

New advanced settings are available to manage Zoom virtual learning sessions with more granular control.

What Is Changing?

Administrators get additional configuration options for Zoom-based virtual learning — better control over session behavior.

Why Should We Care?

Reduces friction in Zoom-based training delivery. Gives admins finer control over the virtual classroom experience.

Risk of Inaction

Zoom session management remains basic. May require manual workarounds for specific settings.

Recommendation Enable if using Zoom
