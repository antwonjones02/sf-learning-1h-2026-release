Change Assignment Due Dates from Team View

Feature #38 of 54

What Is This Feature?

Managers can now change assignment due dates for their team members directly from the Team View page.

What Is Changing?

Due date adjustments — previously requiring admin-level access — can now be done by managers in Team View.

Why Should We Care?

Empowers managers to make real-time adjustments based on workload and priorities without admin support tickets.

Risk of Inaction

Managers must submit requests to admins for due date changes. Slower, more friction.

Recommendation Enable

Impact Minor
