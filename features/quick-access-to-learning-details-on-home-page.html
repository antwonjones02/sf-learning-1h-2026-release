Quick Access to Learning Details on Home Page

Feature #15 of 54

What Is This Feature?

Learners can view additional learning details for courses, programs, classes, and curricula directly on the Learning home page without navigating to the details page.

What Is Changing?

A quick-view panel shows key details (description, duration, prerequisites) inline on the home page.

Why Should We Care?

Reduces navigation clicks and helps learners make faster decisions about what to take next. Improves the self-service learning experience.

Risk of Inaction

Learners continue navigating away from the home page to see details — more clicks, more friction, lower engagement.
