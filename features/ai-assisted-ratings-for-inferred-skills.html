AI-Assisted Ratings for Inferred Skills

Feature #46 of 54

What Is This Feature?

Generative AI can now extract skill proficiency ratings for skills that were inferred through the AI-assisted skills inference feature.

What Is Changing?

AI not only identifies relevant skills for learning content but now also suggests proficiency ratings for each skill.

Why Should We Care?

Accelerates skills taxonomy development at scale. Enables more precise skills-based learning recommendations.

Risk of Inaction

Skills are tagged without proficiency levels — limiting the precision of skills-based recommendations and gap analysis.
