Review AI-Inferred Skills Before Associating

Feature #47 of 54

What Is This Feature?

Administrators can create skills inference requests, monitor progress, and review AI-inferred skills and ratings before associating them with learning items.

What Is Changing?

A human-in-the-loop workflow for AI skills inference — review and approve before skills are linked to content.

Why Should We Care?

Ensures quality control over AI-generated skills associations. Builds trust in automated tagging.

Risk of Inaction

No ability to leverage AI for skills tagging. Manual tagging remains the only option — slow and inconsistent.
