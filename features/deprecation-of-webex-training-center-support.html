Deprecation of Webex Training Center Support

Feature #54 of 54

What Is This Feature?

Support for Webex Training Center as a virtual learning vendor in SAP SuccessFactors Learning is ending November 2026.

What Is Changing?

Webex Training Center will no longer be supported as a virtual learning system vendor. Organizations must migrate to an alternative.

Why Should We Care?

If your organization uses Webex Training Center for virtual classes, you need to migrate to Webex Meetings, Zoom, or Teams.

Risk of Inaction

Virtual learning sessions using Webex Training Center will stop working after November 2026.

Recommendation Migrate VLS vendor
Type Deprecated
Impact Critical
